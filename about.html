Our Legacy of Quality

Who We Are

Restaurant & Hotel Linen Services is a proudly Irish-owned and operated commercial laundry and linen rental service company. We specialize in supply solutions for Ireland's premier hotels, luxury restaurants, spas, and boutique hospitality spaces.

Our luxury minimalist brand identity centers around "Quiet Luxury" and "Impeccable Reliability." We know that crisp, clean linen is not just a utility, but a crucial element of your guest's experience and your business's reputation.

"We allow General Managers and Executive Housekeepers to focus completely on their operations while we guarantee a constant, flawless linen cycle."
